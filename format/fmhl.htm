Notes:
After level 74, if you level up, you'll gain a lot of levels at once up to 88. If you are at level 88 and level up you will go up to 100, then to 108, and so on. Note: Except for Paladins, Commanders use the same type of experience and level upgrade as their Heroes, up to level 65, then their levels are at higher experience points, and do not appear to exceed level 75 regardless of the point value. Paladins have the Learning ability, so their experience points and levels do not match those of their Heroes. This combined with the quirks in the experience/level formula cause their levels to fluctuate wildly at high hero levels. For example, when Sir Mullich is at level 50, his Commander's experience points are negative.
